Creamy White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients | Steps | Tips

Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time 15 min
Servings 2
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Garlic
  • Capsicum
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Prepare white sauce using butter and flour.
  3. Add milk slowly and stir.
  4. Add vegetables and cook.
  5. Mix pasta and cheese.

Tips

Use fresh cream for richer taste.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients | Steps

Prep Time 20 min
Cook Time 40 min
Servings 4
Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani Masala

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken.
  2. Cook rice till 80% done.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Cook on dum.
  5. Serve hot.
